Batman

Who is batman

Batman is a superhero from the fictional city of gotham. He was created by DC comics. He has a sidekick named Robin.

Why bats

In the movie Batman Begins, Bruce chooses the bat for his symbol because he has a bat phobia stemming from a childhood accident. He picks his own most-feared creature and turns it on his enemies, hoping that he will make himself stronger by using a symbol which actually frightens him.
